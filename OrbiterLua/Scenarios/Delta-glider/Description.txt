<h1>Delta-glider scenarios</h1>
<p>The Delta-glider is a futuristic spaceplane design with aerodynamic control surfaces and powerful engines. The DG is a capable allround
vessel ideally suited for novice pilots to practise orbital manoeuvres.</p>

<p>This version has been re-designed and improved, and now includes a variant with air-breathing scramjet engines. The glider is also the first craft to
support a full virtual cockpit implementation.</p>
